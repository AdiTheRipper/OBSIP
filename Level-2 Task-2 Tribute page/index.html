Who is Eren Yaeger?

Eren Yeager is the main protagonist of the Attack on Titan anime and manga series created by Hajime Isayama. He is a member of the Scout Regiment, the military branch dedicated to exploring and reclaiming human territory from the Titans. Eren is determined to rid the world of Titans after witnessing the death of his mother and the destruction of his hometown by them.

During his journey, Eren discovers that he possesses the power of the Titans, which he uses to fight against the Titans and uncover the secrets of their existence. He is a complex character with a strong sense of justice and a desire for revenge against those who have wronged him and his loved ones.

Notable Achievements

Defeating the Female Titan

Eren successfully defeats the Female Titan in a one-on-one battle, demonstrating his strength and determination as a soldier.

Saving Mikasa

Eren saves Mikasa from the hands of human traffickers, solidifying their bond and friendship.

Working with Levi

Eren is chosen to work with Levi and his squad due to his unique abilities, and together they successfully capture the Female Titan.

The Attack Titan

Eren Yeager is the current holder of the Attack Titan, a powerful Titan with the ability to see into the memories of its past users and manipulate memories of others.

The Founding Titan

The Founding Titan is one of the Nine Titans, and it possesses the ability to control the memories and minds of Eldians. It is said that the Founding Titan was the first Titan to exist, and that it possesses incredible power.

Rage

Eren's intense rage towards the Titans and those who have wronged him fuels much of his actions throughout the series. His anger often leads him to act recklessly, but it also gives him the strength to stand up to his enemies and fight for what he believes in.



The Attack on Titan series has come to it's fourth season, leaving behind a legacy of intense action, emotional moments, and unforgettable characters. Eren Yaeger's journey may have ended, but his legacy lives on in the hearts of fans all around the world.
